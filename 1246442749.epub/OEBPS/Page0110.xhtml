Plate 61. Hairy Cell Leukemia: Blood Smears  
Selected cells in blood smears from a patient with hairy cell leukemia. These cells have veillike cytoplasmic extrusions and  
delicate threadlike filaments. Hairy cells tend to push neighboring cells away or aside, leaving clear spaces around the hairy  
cell. One cell has prominent azure granules and a few hairlike projections.
