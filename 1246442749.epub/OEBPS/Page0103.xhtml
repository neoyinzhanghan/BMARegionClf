Plate 54. Micromegakaryoblasts in Blood and Marrow Smears  
of Megakaryoblastic Crisis of CML  
Selected cells from patient with megakaryoblastic crisis of chronic myelogenous leukemia. Variant forms of micromegakaryoblasts.  
Nuclei are usually small and single but one cell has two nuclei. In most cells, granular cytoplasmic blebs (which represent early  
platelet formation) are noted.
